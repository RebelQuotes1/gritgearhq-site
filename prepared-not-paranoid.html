Prepared, Not Paranoid

A short, gritty field guide for people who want to be ready for real-world disruptions without turning preparedness into fear, obsession, or cosplay.

What this is (and what it isn’t)

This isn’t a survival fantasy. It’s a practical reset for normal people who’ve lived long enough to know the world usually breaks quietly.

  • Power outages
  • Cold snaps
  • Supply hiccups
  • Unexpected downtime

The goal isn’t domination. It’s margin — enough warmth, food, sleep, and calm to make good decisions.
