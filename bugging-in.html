🧱 Step 3: Reinforce Entry Points

A cheap lock is a welcome mat for thieves. Install high-security deadbolts, reinforced strike plates, and door armor kits. Window bars aren’t crazy—they’re smart.

🕯️ Step 4: Blackout and Black In

If they can’t see you, they can’t hurt you. Blackout curtains. Window foil. Paint. Use light discipline like your life depends on it—because it might.
