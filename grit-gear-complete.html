🔥 Les Stroud Survival Story

If He Can Do It, So Can I

I was 21 when I first saw Les Stroud — calm, deliberate, alone in the wild — and something in me lit up. It wasn’t inspiration. It was a challenge...

So I packed up the dumbest assortment of camping gear imaginable — gimmicky gadgets, canned food, an unrated sleeping bag...

That longfire was the only thing that kept me alive that night. And Les Stroud — his voice, his pacing, his calm in the storm — had trained me without knowing it.
